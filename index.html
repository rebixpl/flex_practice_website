Hey there, I'm Mariusz Pudzianowski

Mariusz Zbigniew Pudzianowski , known in Poland as "Pudzian" and "Dominator" is a Polish former strongman competitor and current mixed martial artist.

He is widely considered one of the greatest strongmen in the history of the sport.

During his career as a strongman, Pudzianowski won five World's Strongest Man titles, the most in history.
